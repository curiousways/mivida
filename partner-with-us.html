Building confidence through collaboration

Your clients trust you to help guide them through their most important decisions, from business structuring to estate planning and tax strategies. We’re here to work alongside you to provide a comprehensive wealth management service that complements the excellent guidance you already provide.

Stronger Together

The people who come to us often have complex financial lives spanning business interests, family trusts and long-standing professional relationships. We believe the best outcomes don’t come from working in isolation, but from understanding how all the pieces fit together.

Our expertise in long-term planning and investment strategy dovetails seamlessly with the work you’re already doing. We work with Accountants and Solicitors to support, collaborate, and occasionally challenge diplomatically and sensitively, helping create joined-up professional advice that pulls in the same direction. Working together, clients feel more confident and make better decisions.

When you introduce a client to Mivida, we see it as the beginning of a partnership designed to support them for the long term.

Professional relationships

Complex decisions often require more than one perspective. That’s why we often join clients for key meetings, to bring a wider financial view into the room and ensure every angle is properly considered.

Our role is to complement yours: making connections across tax, legal, and investment issues so nothing is overlooked and clients feel fully supported.
We know professional trust is built over time; we work with you in a way that seeks to help strengthen existing relationships and create a smoother experience for your clients.
If you value coordinated advice that puts clients at the centre, we’d be glad to explore how we can work together.

Building opportunities

Our growth has always come through trusted referrals and introductions, and our reputation depends on working with like-minded professionals. That’s why we seek partnerships only with established firms who share our client-first values and culture of excellence.

We typically collaborate with advisers who are experienced in supporting individuals and families over the long term, and who recognise the value that close, complementary professional relationships bring to their clients.

Through our True Wealth Management approach, we can help your clients achieve financial clarity, all while strengthening and deepening the relationships you already hold with them.

What Makes Us Different

We're independently owned and one of only a select number of Chartered wealth management firms in the UK, combining true independence with the highest professional standards. Our advisers are never paid based on what they recommend to you, their compensation has no connection to the advice they give. This means every recommendation is based solely on what serves your situation best, not what generates fees for us. You get unbiased expertise and strategies built entirely around your needs.
