Grow your career
with Mivida

We are a privately owned, wholly independent, chartered private wealth management firm. Anyone wanting to join our team not only needs to hold appropriate qualifications and have suitable experience, but must be willing to embrace our team approach.

Our independence means we answer only to our clients, not to external shareholders or product providers. That commitment runs through everything we do,  including how we build and support our team.

Joining Mivida means more than holding the right qualifications and experience. We look for people who share our belief that financial planning should make life better, not more complicated.
People who thrive on collaboration, who care about the long-term wellbeing of clients and take pride in maintaining the highest professional standards.

Working at Mivida

Our culture is built on the same values we extend to our clients; on care, collaboration, curiosity, and commitment. We invest in professional growth, offering the opportunity to pursue additional qualifications, develop specialist expertise, and progress within the firm. Every role is important: from advisers and client support, to technical specialists and those in a central role, we work as one team to deliver exceptional outcomes. What sets us apart is our long-term approach. Just as we commit to being partners for life with our clients, we aim to build lasting careers with our people, giving them the tools and support to thrive.

Careers

We build in-depth relationships with our clients and understand their life ambitions, challenges and needs. The nature of our highly personal, expert service means that our firm’s growth is facilitated through key relationship referrals and introductions. Our staff stay with us over the long term and that is something our clients appreciate.

We always welcome contact from well qualified individuals looking to take the next step in their career.

Chartered or certified financial planners

Already qualified to level 6, you will be keen to take on additional qualifications in specialist areas, with our support. You should have a successful track record in providing high quality financial planning advice to High Net Worth individuals, businesses and trustees.

Importantly you will want to work within a team of highly qualified people to produce top quality financial planning reports and advice for our clients.

Financial Ombudsman Service

Paraplanners are an integral part of the KMD team. Supporting our Chartered Financial Planners, you will be responsible for the technical research that underpins our recommendations to clients. Plus the production of engaging financial planning reports.

Qualified to Level 4 with a minimum of two years’ experience, you will have ambitions to progress your professional qualifications, with our support.

What Makes Us Different

We're independently owned and one of only a select number of Chartered wealth management firms in the UK, combining true independence with the highest professional standards. Our advisers are never paid based on what they recommend to you, their compensation has no connection to the advice they give. This means every recommendation is based solely on what serves your situation best, not what generates fees for us. You get unbiased expertise and strategies built entirely around your needs.
