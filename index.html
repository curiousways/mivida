How we Help
At Mivida, we understand that your finances are not simply about money; they’re your ambitions, security and legacy. We go beyond traditional wealth management, becoming your trusted partner through every chapter of your financial story. We bring clarity to complex decisions and care to every conversation. Whether you're building wealth for tomorrow or protecting what you've already created, we craft strategies shaped entirely by your unique circumstances.
Your path is your own, but you don’t have to walk it alone.

Clarity in Complexity

Financial life can feel overwhelming when pensions, investments, and savings are spread across multiple providers. We gather everything together, organise it clearly and show you exactly where you stand. No more guessing about forgotten policies or wondering if you're on track. We transform scattered financial pieces into one comprehensive picture that makes sense.

How we Help

Confidence in the Future

Through thoughtful planning and careful forecasting, we help you move forward with certainty. We model different scenarios for your future, from unexpected challenges to retirement dreams. You'll understand what might happen and how to prepare for multiple possibilities. Partnering with us ensures you have robust plans that adapt as life unfolds.

Who we Are

A Human Approach

Money conversations should feel reassuring, never intimidating or overwhelming. We speak your language, not industry jargon. We listen to your concerns and explain options clearly. Most importantly, we remember the person behind the plan: you. The hopes you're building toward, worries keeping you awake at night, and dreams we're here to help protect and bring to life. We help you find the delicate balance between preparing for your future, experiencing your present and fully embracing life along the way.

Who we work with

We typically work with individuals, couples and families who have built, or are in the process of building around £10m in total assets. Many of our clients are entrepreneurs, business owners and equity partners in professional services firms. They value our thoughtful guidance, long-term perspective and a relationship with experts in a firm that genuinely understands them.
